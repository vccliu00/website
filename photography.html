About Me



Hi! My name is Victoria!

I am a beginner hobbist photographer located in the Portland area who started in early 2021. I mainly take pictures with my Nikon d5500 and my Iphone XS. My favorite things to take pictures of are plants and animals!


My goal for 2022 is to continue taking pictures all throughout the year! I hope to capture more moments on film.
